Otae san detector

Shimura Tae (志村 妙 Shimura Tae), or just Otae (お妙) (the "お" (O) is an honorific used to refer to women), is the older sister of Shimura Shinpachi. She works at Snack Smile as a cabaret hostess to keep the family doujo Koudoukan going. This is a deep learning model created to detect Otae san. I've heavily used the fast.ai library and learned to make this in lesson 1 of fast.ai deep learning 1 course.


Architecture: resnet34
Training data: I collected by taking screenshots while watching the anime Gintama There were total 1600 images with 700 of them marked as "otae" for the training.
Request sent by: jquery and ajax
Backend: Flask
